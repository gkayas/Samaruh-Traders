## Key Terms Explained (for non-Amazon readers)

- **Anchor Monthly Units**: A conservative estimate of how many units a product sells per month, based on market performance indicators and third-party sales estimators.
- **Monthly Net Revenue**: Estimated revenue after Amazon selling fees, fulfillment costs, logistics, and operating buffers.
- **Max COGS (40%)**: The maximum manufacturer cost per unit (FOB Bangladesh) that still allows a minimum 40% net margin.
- **Confidence**: Indicates the reliability of the estimate based on data availability and consistency across sources.
