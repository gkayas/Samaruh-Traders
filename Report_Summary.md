## How to Read This Table

- **Anchor Monthly Units**  
  Conservative estimate of monthly unit sales, anchored primarily to **Helium 10 Free** and cross-checked with **Jungle Scout** and **AMZScout** free estimators.

- **Monthly Net Revenue**  
  Approximate net revenue after Amazon fees, PPC buffer, and logistics, based on conservative assumptions.

- **Max COGS @40%**  
  Maximum manufacturer buy price (FOB Bangladesh) that still allows a **40% net margin** under the current cost model.

- **Confidence**  
  Reflects data completeness and reliability:
  - *Medium*: Anchored to Helium 10 Free output
  - *Low–Medium*: Based on conservative free-tool triangulation

## Executive Takeaways

- Products with **Max COGS ≥ $5** are viable **only if** Bangladesh manufacturing efficiencies are achieved.
- A **20% referral fee + 40–50% margin target** significantly tightens acceptable supplier pricing.
- Among reviewed products, **B07V7BZZ1Q** shows the strongest balance of demand and pricing headroom.
- Other ASINs require **price differentiation or bundling** to safely meet margin targets.
